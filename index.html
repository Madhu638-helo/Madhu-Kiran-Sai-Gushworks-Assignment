Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of a laser cutter for sheet metal?

It is designed to cut various types of sheet metal with precision, allowing for intricate designs and shapes that are essential in manufacturing processes.

What are the benefits of using aluminium tubing in manufacturing?

Aluminium tubing offers excellent strength-to-weight ratio, corrosion resistance, and ease of fabrication making it ideal for a wide range of manufacturing applications.

How is aluminium tubing produced?

Aluminium tubing is produced through extrusion or drawing processes where heated aluminium billets are forced through shaped dies to create tubes of precise dimensions.

What are the common applications of aluminium tubing?

Common applications include aerospace structures, automotive parts, HVAC systems, bicycle frames, medical equipment, and construction scaffolding.

Can aluminium tubing be customised?

Yes, aluminium tubing can be customised in terms of diameter, wall thickness, alloy composition, temper, and surface finish to meet specific engineering requirements.

Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing
Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution

Precision Extrusion

Twin-screw extruders process the HDPE compound at precise temperatures ensuring uniform melt flow and consistent wall thickness throughout the entire pipe length.

  • Temperature-controlled zones
  • Automated melt pressure monitoring

Controlled Cooling

Water bath cooling systems ensure gradual, uniform cooling of pipes preventing internal stress, warping, or dimensional variation.

  • Multi-stage water bath system
  • Temperature-controlled cooling

Precision Sizing

Advanced haul-off units pull the pipe at exact speeds while sizing units ensure precise outer diameters and wall thicknesses.

  • Laser diameter measurement
  • Automatic dimensional correction

Quality Control

Every pipe passes through rigorous hydrostatic pressure tests, dimensional checks, and material property verification before leaving our facility.

  • 100% hydrostatic pressure testing
  • ISO 4427 compliance check

Inkjet Marking

Automated inkjet printers mark every pipe with product specifications, certifications, batch numbers, and manufacturing date for full traceability.

  • UV-resistant ink marking
  • Full traceability codes

Precision Cutting

Planetary cutters provide clean, burr-free cuts at exact lengths with automated length measurement ensuring every pipe meets specified tolerances.

  • ±2mm length tolerance
  • Burr-free cut ends

Packaging & Dispatch

Pipes are bundled, wrapped, and labelled according to customer requirements and international shipping standards for safe transportation.

  • Custom bundling options
  • Export-quality packaging
